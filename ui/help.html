Dynamic Pricing

The module tracks post occupancy and, under high load, sends an MQTT debit coefficient (the price list is not changed).

CRM logic

  • Polls post-states and counts busy posts for the selected car wash.
  • When the threshold is reached — publishes {prefix}/{serial}/set/surge with the coefficient (via message-processor, system account).
  • When occupancy drops — sends reset (active: 0, coefficient: 1).
  • Prices in CRM and on the panel are not overwritten.

MQTT payload

{ "coefficient": 1.10, "active": 1, "until_balance_zero": 1 }

The coefficient applies on the device until the current session balance reaches zero (panel-side logic).

Car wash integration

Full firmware specification — CLIENT.md in the module repository.

Settings

  • wash_id — car wash.
  • busy_threshold — minimum busy posts (e.g. 9 of 10).
  • price_increase_percent — percent → coefficient (10% = 1.10).
  • poll_interval — poll interval (from 5 sec, step 5 sec).
